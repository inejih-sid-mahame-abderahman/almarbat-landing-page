Frequently Asked Questions

Clear systematic architectural feedback resolving operational deployment concerns.

How do I purchase livestock on Almarbat?
Select your animal from the open marketplace, click reserve to hold the resource, and proceed to send the currency value via your preferred Mauritanian digital wallet application.
What payment apps are currently accepted?
We accept verified receipt configurations originating from Bankily, Masrivi, Bimbank, and Sedad transaction systems.
How does the Group Purchase mechanism function?
System administrators initiate group pools for massive livestock items. Users can acquire defined fractional quotas until the collective funding parameters reach 100%.
What if a designated group pool fails to meet goals?
If the configuration window expires without fulfilling the target requirements, all user balances return to their source wallets automatically.
Are sellers verified inside the platform?
Yes, all listing agents must present official national registration cards and valid geographical location proof sequences before accessing sales channels.
How long does administrative verification take?
Our backoffice validation groups review transactions 24/7. Typically, authorizations clear between 15 to 45 minutes from initial upload timestamps.
How is the livestock delivery handled safely?
Registered logistics drivers execute livestock transport inside adapted trucks designed to ensure animal health remains protected throughout regional transits.
What is the utility of the security QR code sequence?
The dynamic QR signature sequence ensures security. The delivery carrier only receives final validation status upon physical inspection approval by the recipient.
Can I inspect the animal before validation?
Absolutely. Our architectural frameworks guarantee that buyers can review structural livestock parameters upon physical container handoffs before displaying confirmation keys.
Are there structural fees for platform buyers?
Account registration is fully free. Minimal transactional service fees apply depending entirely on your chosen logistics matrix distance calculations.
How does fraud detection guard payment entries?
Algorithmic processors evaluate structural image data variables to catch identical receipt duplicate forging patterns instantly.
Can I manage my own logistics fleet as a seller?
Yes, sellers can select custom delivery configuration rules inside their control panel dashboards easily.
Which regions are actively serviced inside Mauritania?
We maintain fully integrated logistical network systems across Nouakchott, Rosso, Néma, Nouadhibou, and surrounding rural sectors.
Is offline access supported on the mobile app?
Yes, internal databases store operational transaction records locally to allow smooth monitoring even during cellular service interruptions.
Who handles complaints inside the platform ecosystem?
Our dedicated administrative support operations center mediates disputes instantly to secure structural safety across all user tiers.
