Frequently Asked QuestionsQuestions Fréquentesالأسئلة الأكثر شيوعاً

How do I purchase livestock on Almarbat?

Select an animal from the app, tap Reserve to lock it, then transfer payment via Bankily, Masrivi, Bimbank, or Sedad. Upload your receipt screenshot and our team verifies it within 15–45 minutes.

What payment apps are accepted?

We accept Bankily, Masrivi, Bimbank, and Sedad — all major Mauritanian digital wallets.

How does Group Purchase work?

Admin users create pools for large animals like camels. You buy a fractional share (1/2, 1/4, etc.). Once the pool is full, the order is placed. If it doesn't fill in time, everyone gets a full refund automatically.

What if the group pool doesn't fill up?

If the pool's time window expires without reaching the funding target, all contributions are refunded automatically to each participant's wallet. Zero risk.

Are sellers verified?

Yes. Every seller must submit their national ID and proof of location to our admin team before they can list anything. No verification, no access.

How long does admin payment verification take?

Our team reviews receipts 24/7. Most verifications complete within 15 to 45 minutes from upload.

How is delivery handled safely?

Registered, verified drivers handle all deliveries using vehicles suited for livestock transport. You track your delivery live, and the handover is confirmed with a QR code scan.

What is the QR code used for?

The QR code is your unique delivery confirmation key. The driver can only mark the delivery complete after you show them your code — after you've inspected the animal first.

Can I inspect the animal before confirming?

Absolutely. The QR confirmation only happens after you inspect and are satisfied. You're never forced to accept an animal that doesn't match its listing.

Are there fees for buyers?

Registration is completely free. Small logistics fees apply depending on the delivery distance, shown transparently before you confirm your order.

Which regions does Almarbat serve?

We currently serve Nouakchott, Rosso, Néma, Nouadhibou, and surrounding rural areas. We're expanding steadily across Mauritania.

Does the app work with poor connectivity?

Yes. The app caches your orders and data locally, so you can browse and track even when you have limited or no signal. It syncs when connectivity returns.
